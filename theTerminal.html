The Terminal

Released year: 2004

Viktor Navorski gets stranded at an airport when a war rages in his country. He is forced by the officials to stay at the airport until his original identity is confirmed.

Director:

Steven Spielberg

Story By:

Andrew Niccol; Sacha Gervasi

Cast:

Tom Hanks as Viktor Navorski

Catherine Zeta-Jones as Amelia Warren

Stanley Tucci as Frank Dixon

Chi McBride as Joe Mulroy
